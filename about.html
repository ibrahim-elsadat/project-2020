Quality Policy

Velmed Hospitals is committed to provide empathetic collaborative and comprehensive health care services to its patient and guest at an affordable rate considering all section of its populace. Associates of Velmed Hospitals are committed to continuous quality improvement in facility and services. This policy is on regular review for its suitability with changing the environment and its guests need.

Our Quality Team is a high powered committee comprising of Senior Medical Staff and Senior Management of the hospital. The committee reviews data and reports regularly and makes suitable recommendations. The committee also monitors and analyzes all Patient Safety and Infection Control data of the hospital to ensure patient and staff safety. Velmed hospital actively monitors the following quality indicators regularly:

  • Patient safety
  • Infection control
  • Medication safety
  • Emergency response
  • Patient satisfaction
  • Internal and External Quality Controls

Our laboratories have both internal and external quality controls in ensuring the diagnostic quality, and follow daily QC (Quality Check) on each equipment as part of our Internal Quality Control Check.
